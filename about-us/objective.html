Objective

The department of labour welfare and administration was formed with the following objectives.

Objective of the Department

  • Labour Welfare and Administration of Labor Laws.
  • Inspection of Safety measures of Factories & Boilers and Administration of Factories & Boilers Acts & Rules.
  • Social Security Schemes for Industrial Workers, like E.S.I.
  • Employment and Vocational Guidance to the Youth.
  • Adjudication of Industrial Disputes.
  • Child Labour Welfare.
